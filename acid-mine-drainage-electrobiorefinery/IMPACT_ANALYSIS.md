## 7. Key Assumptions & Uncertainties

| Assumption | Sensitivity | Risk |
|------------|-------------|------|
| REE concentration in AMD (100–600 µg/L) | High — sites with <50 µg/L may not be REE-profitable | Diversified revenue (metals + acid + aggregate + carbon) covers low-REE sites |
| MFC power output (0.3–2.0 kWh/m³) | Medium — biofilm stability over 10+ yr unproven | Backup PV + grid connection; MFC is self-regenerating |
| Lanmodulin column longevity (500–1,000 cycles) | Medium — field degradation unknown | Column replacement is low-cost ($1–2.5K/6mo); peptide cost declining |
| Carbon credit pricing ($40–120/t CO₂) | High — voluntary carbon market volatile | Carbon is bonus revenue, not primary economics |
| Regulatory approval for bioagents | Medium — LanM vesicles, Desulfovibrio | Non-living vesicles, non-pathogenic, auxotrophic; biosafety Level 1 |
| Site access and permitting | Medium — abandoned mine land ownership | Partner with state AML agencies who own many sites |
