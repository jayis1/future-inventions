| Scale | Dust avoided/yr | Premature deaths prevented/yr | PM₂.₅ reduction in downwind cities |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| 1M ha | 10–18 Mt | 2,000–4,000 | 0.5–2 µg/m³ |

**Dust reduction mechanism:** Biocrust covers and stabilizes bare soil surface, reducing wind entrainment by 80–95%. Even 1 mm of cyanobacterial crust reduces threshold friction velocity for dust mobilization by 40–60%.

## 9. Scaling Projections

| Year | Cumulative ha restored | CO₂/yr (Mt) | Jobs | Cost/ha | Cost per t CO₂ |
|------|------------------------|-------------|------|---------|----------------|
| 2030 | 10K–50K (pilot) | 0.02–0.4 | 500–2K | $200–400 | $2.5–10 |
| 2035 | 1–5M (early scale) | 2–40 | 5K–20K | $100–200 | $1.5–5 |
| 2040 | 50–100M (full scale) | 100–800 | 50K–200K | $55–143 | $0.70–1.80 |
| 2045 | 200–300M | 400–2,400 | 100K–500K | $55–120 | $0.70–1.50 |
| 2050 | 500M | 1,000–4,000 | 200K–1M | $40–100 | $0.50–1.20 |

**Scaling logic:** Cost/ha declines 40–60% from pilot to ubiquity via (1) drone cost reduction (manufacturing scale, longer life), (2) capsule manufacturing automation, (3) strain improvement (higher survival → lower density needed), (4) self-propagation reducing reapplication.

## 10. Risks to Impact Realization

| Risk | Impact on projections | Mitigation |
|------|----------------------|-----------|
| Regulatory delay (GM microbe release) | +3–5 years to Phase 3 | Start with wild-type strains; parallel regulatory engagement |
| Climate change outpacing restoration | Higher mortality, slower spread | Strain engineering for higher heat/drought tolerance; adaptive deployment timing |
| Funding gap ($5–15B for full scale) | Slower scaling | Carbon credit pre-sales; UNCCD/GCF funding; public-private partnerships |
| Manufacturing bottleneck | Capsule supply < demand | Modular, distributed manufacturing; open-source design for local production |
| Political instability in target regions | Deployment interruptions | Multi-region strategy; mobile dock stations; UN coordination |
