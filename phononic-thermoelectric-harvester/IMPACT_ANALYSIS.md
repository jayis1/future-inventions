## 5. Material Sustainability

| Material | Global Supply (kt/yr) | PTH Demand at 50M modules (t) | Fraction of Supply |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tin (Sn) | 300 | 7,500 | 2.5% |
| Selenium (Se) | 3 | 2,500 | 83% ⚠️ |
| Molybdenum (Mo) | 250 | 1,500 | 0.6% |

**Selenium is the binding constraint.** Mitigations:
- Mg₂Si/TiO₂ phononic filter alternative eliminates Se in the filter layer
- Se recycling loop (>95% recovery at EOL)
- Se-free thermoelectric alternatives (Mg₂Si, ZnSb) as second-generation materials
- At 50M modules with Se recycling, net virgin Se demand drops to ~400 t/yr (13% of supply)
