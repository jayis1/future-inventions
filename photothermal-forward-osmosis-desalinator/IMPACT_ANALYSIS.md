## 3. Climate & Energy Impact

| Metric | SWRO Displaced (m³/day) | Electricity Avoided (TWh/yr) | CO₂ Avoided (Mt/yr) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Early Scale (2034) | 1.5M | 1.9 | 0.9–2.8 |
| Mid Scale (2037) | 15M | 19 | 9.5–28 |
| Full Scale (2040) | 60M | 77 | 38–115 |

Assumptions: SWRO energy 3.0–5.5 kWh/m³; grid carbon intensity 0.4–0.7 kg CO₂/kWh; PFOD replaces 100% of displaced volume (zero electricity for water production). PFOD modules themselves are carbon-negative over lifecycle (embodied carbon ~50 kg CO₂/module, offset by avoided SWRO emissions within first 3 days of operation).

**Lifecycle Carbon Assessment:**
- Embodied carbon per 5 m³/day module: ~45–55 kg CO₂e (hydrogel 8 kg, membrane 2 kg, glass/steel 30 kg, plastic housing 10 kg)
- Carbon payback period: **2–4 days** of operation (displacing grid SWRO)
- 15-year module lifetime carbon savings: **8,000–25,000 kg CO₂e** per module

## Summary

PFOD offers the highest ratio of impact-to-investment among proposed water technologies: zero operating energy, lowest LCOE, no grid dependency, modular scalability, and applicability across 80+ countries. At full scale (2040), it enables safe water access for 500M+ people, avoids 38–115 Mt CO₂/yr, saves $15–55B/yr in operating costs, and pays back its embodied carbon in less than one week of operation.
