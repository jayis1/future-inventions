## 2. PRN Intervention Targets

### Layer 1: BeeShield™ Probiotic
| Metric | Baseline | PRN Target | Mechanism |
|--------|---------|------------|-----------|
| Neonicotinoid internal burden | Full dose retained | 5–20× reduction | CYP9Q4 hydroxylation |
| Varroa mite reproduction | Uncontrolled | 60–80% suppression | dsRNA (RmVgR) |
| DWV viral load | Endemic, high | 85–95% reduction | dsRNA (capsid) |
| Nosema spore load | 30–50% (fumagillin) | 70–90% reduction | Apidaecin AMP |
| Colony annual loss | 30–40% | <10–15% | Integrated effect |

### Layer 2: DriftShield Hedgerow
| Metric | Baseline | PRN Target |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Drift interception at field margin | 10–20% (buffer strip) | 80–95% |
| Neonicotinoid soil half-life | 1000+ days | <30 days |
| CO₂ sequestration (hedgerow biomass) | 0 | 0.5–2 t/ha/yr |

### Layer 3: CorridorMapper
| Metric | Baseline | PRN Target |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Forage gap detection resolution | 30 m (satellite only) | 2–5 cm (drone) |
| Continuous bloom days | 60–90 | >120 |
| Deployment rate | 2–5 ha/day (manual) | 200–500 ha/hour (drone) |
| Forage connectivity (max gap) | Unmanaged | <2 km (honeybee), <500 m (solitary) |

### Layer 4: HiveSentinel
| Metric | Baseline | PRN Target |
